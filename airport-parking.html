🚌 About Park & Ride

Park and ride airport parking is the most popular choice among UK holidaymakers, and it's easy to see why. By parking at a car park slightly further from the terminal — typically a 5–20 minute shuttle bus ride away — you can save a significant amount compared to on-airport alternatives. The shuttle buses are regular, comfortable, and usually run 24 hours a day. Most park and ride sites are secured with CCTV, barrier access, and on-site security, so your car is in safe hands while you're away. If you're travelling as a family with lots of luggage, or heading off for a fortnight in the sun, park and ride gives you excellent value without sacrificing peace of mind.

🤝 About Meet & Greet

Meet and greet parking is the premium end of airport parking, and once you've tried it, it's hard to go back. You simply drive to the terminal, a uniformed valet meets you at the drop-off zone, checks your car over, and drives it to a secure compound. On your return, your car is brought back to the terminal for you. There's no hunting for a shuttle bus with tired children in tow — you just walk from your car straight into check-in. Meet and greet is particularly popular for early morning flights, business travellers, and anyone who values their time. When you book with Holiday Extras, all our meet and greet operators are vetted, insured, and approved — so your car is always in safe hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking airport parking with Holiday Extras.

As a general rule of thumb, the earlier you book your airport parking, the better the price you'll get. We recommend booking as soon as you've confirmed your flight — even if that's months in advance. Prices at many car parks can be 40–70% cheaper when booked well in advance compared to booking on the day. Most of our bookings include free cancellation, so there's no risk in booking early. If your plans change, you can cancel or amend your booking free of charge up to the day before travel on most products.

Yes, almost always. Airport parking — like flights and hotels — operates on dynamic pricing, meaning the price increases as availability decreases and your travel date approaches. Booking 4–8 weeks in advance can save you 40–70% compared to booking on the day of travel. Our comparison tool shows you prices across multiple operators, so you can find the best deal for your dates. We also regularly offer exclusive promotional rates to customers who book early, so it always pays to plan ahead.

On-airport parking refers to car parks that are owned and operated directly by the airport — typically the multi-storey or surface car parks right next to the terminal building. They're the most convenient option but also the most expensive. Off-airport parking refers to independently operated car parks located near the airport, usually a short shuttle bus ride away. Off-airport options are generally significantly cheaper and often just as secure, CCTV-monitored, and well run as their on-airport equivalents. Many of our customers are pleasantly surprised by the quality of off-airport car parks — and the savings they make.

The vast majority of airport parking products sold through Holiday Extras include free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled drop-off time. This means you can book with complete confidence, knowing that if your plans change — whether due to a cancelled flight, a change of holiday dates, or any other reason — you can cancel and receive a full refund. Cancellation policies vary slightly by product, so always check the terms when booking. Any products that don't include free cancellation are clearly marked, and the cancellation terms are shown before you confirm your purchase.

Flight delays are an unfortunate reality of air travel, but they shouldn't cost you extra. If your return flight is delayed and you arrive back later than your booked return time, most car parks will honour your booking and release your car without charging extra — as long as you call ahead to notify them. Holiday Extras' customer service team is available 24/7 to help you manage any changes caused by delays. We strongly recommend saving the car park's direct number (provided in your booking confirmation) so you can contact them directly if needed. For added peace of mind, consider adding our parking delay cover at the time of booking.
